Abstract Flow Art

Digital Painting Microsoft Paint August 2025

An exploration of organic flowing forms created with deliberate simplicity. Using Microsoft Paint's brush and ellipse tools, this piece demonstrates how creative limitations can spark innovative solutions.

The composition features dynamic blue curves flowing through the upper area, contrasted by geometric red shapes that create visual tension. Green accent strokes provide color balance while purple finishing touches add depth and sophistication.
